Fake News detection

Fake news can be a result of misinformation, or it can be an intentional attempt to intentionally mislead people. Now it has become harder and harder to recognize whether the news is legitimate news from fake news as social media has grown a lot. But here come a machine project base on fake news detection,where i train my models to analyzing vast amounts of data, and identifies patterns also provides an outcomes that are based on those patterns using natural language processing(NLP).The libraries use in this project are; NLTK, Scikit-Learn e.t.c
